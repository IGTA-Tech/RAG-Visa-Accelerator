Purpose & context Sherrod operates a multi-brand immigration services business specializing in agent-based O-1 visa petitions as an alternative to the H-1B lottery system. The business includes four main brands: Innovative Automations (AI/ML tech focus), Innovative Global Talent Agency/IGTA (sports and entertainment), Aventus Visa Agents (general professional services), and Sherrod Sports Visas. With a background as a federal litigator and coder, Sherrod has achieved a 94% approval rate across 500+ cases and regularly speaks at ALIA conferences. The core value proposition centers on agent-based petition structures that provide extraordinary flexibility - approved professionals can work for multiple employers simultaneously, start businesses, switch jobs without refiling, and maintain visa status independent of any single employer. This addresses major pain points in the traditional H-1B system including lottery uncertainty, employer dependency, and recent regulatory changes imposing substantial filing costs. The business targets high-skilled international professionals, particularly from India, China, Taiwan, Eastern Europe, UAE, and Qatar, as well as H-1B holders in the US seeking greater career flexibility. Key collaborators include Ryan (Boolean search specialist), Ankit, and Eugene (email responses), with development work handled by team members like Arun. The business operates through a systematic lead-to-client pipeline: lead capture → AI evaluation → consultation → program enrollment, with all processes designed for scalability and automation. Current state Sherrod is actively scaling operations through multiple parallel initiatives. The lead generation system processes candidates from a database of 4,500+ job candidates across 97 Google Sheets, using AI-powered scoring through OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini to automatically assess O-1 visa qualification against the 8 USCIS criteria. The system assigns candidates to three tiers: READY (immediate filing eligible), ACCELERATOR (6-8 months evidence building needed), and NURTURE (longer-term development required). Current service offerings include three main tiers: O-1 Ready Filing ($10,000-$15,000), O-1 Profile Building/Accelerator programs ($1,500-$2,000 monthly or $10,000-$15,000 total), and Petitioner Services Only ($2,000 for clients with their own attorneys). The business is implementing comprehensive website redesigns across all brands, with specific focus on the Innovative Automations site restructuring around AI/ML Talent, AI/ML Employers, Venture Gallery, and Prototypes/Building sections. Marketing efforts involve LinkedIn automation through Dux Soup, Meta advertising campaigns targeting specific geographic regions, and partnership development with companies like Nexrizen for talent validation and deployment opportunities. The business maintains active consultation booking through Cal.com with separate calendars for different team members and service types. On the horizon Major upcoming initiatives include launching the O1DMatch platform - a two-sided marketplace connecting O-1 candidates with employers through identity masking technology. This comprehensive system will integrate with existing scoring APIs, document upload systems, five-tier interest letter commitment levels, and lawyer directory features, built on Supabase/Vercel/Next.js infrastructure. Sherrod is developing the Professional Deployers Association as a nonprofit certification program that will provide additional O-1 evidence while generating business visibility. Partnership expansion includes exploring staffing/recruiting relationships, white-label service offerings, and integration with infrastructure companies for talent deployment. The business is preparing for equity crowdfunding through StartEngine, positioning the company around the massive underserved market of H-1B lottery rejects, proven success metrics, and multiple revenue streams including employer placement fees. Future scaling involves voice agent systems with comprehensive RAG knowledge bases and automated petition drafting capabilities. Key learnings & principles Agent-based petition structures provide significantly more career flexibility than traditional employer-sponsored visas, allowing multiple employer relationships and business ownership rights that H-1B status prohibits. This positioning resonates strongly with high-skilled professionals seeking portfolio careers and entrepreneurial opportunities. The AI evaluation system at aivisaevaluations.online serves as an effective lead magnet, providing immediate value while qualifying prospects for appropriate service tiers. Automated scoring against O-1 criteria using actual resume content achieves 85-90% accuracy and dramatically improves lead qualification efficiency. Educational, consultative messaging consistently outperforms direct sales approaches in LinkedIn outreach and content marketing. Positioning as industry experts and researchers rather than vendors builds trust and generates higher-quality conversations with prospects. The three-tier service model effectively segments the market: immediate filing for qualified candidates, structured evidence-building programs for emerging professionals, and petitioner-only services for those with existing legal representation. This approach maximizes addressable market while maintaining service quality. Approach & patterns Lead generation operates through multiple channels: MyVisaJobs scraping, LinkedIn automation, Meta advertising, and partnership referrals. All leads flow through Go High Level CRM with automated routing to aivisaevaluations.online for qualification scoring. Content creation follows a systematic approach emphasizing case studies, success stories, and educational resources rather than promotional material. LinkedIn posts and articles position Sherrod as a thought leader in immigration law and AI automation, targeting both talent and employer audiences. Partnership development focuses on mutual value creation rather than transactional relationships. Sherrod emphasizes natural business opportunities flowing from collaborations, positioning partners as talent validators and industry leaders rather than simple referral sources. Website optimization follows a dual-audience approach, clearly separating pathways for international talent seeking visas versus employers needing talent solutions. All forms collect minimal information (first name, last name, email, descriptor) while capturing detailed tracking data for lead attribution. Tools & resources Core technology stack includes Google Sheets for candidate database management, OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for AI scoring and petition drafting, Go High Level for CRM and marketing automation, Cal.com for consultation booking, and Stripe for payment processing.
