# AGENT-BASED O-1 VISA BENEFITS - COMPLETE RAG

## WHAT IS AGENT-BASED O-1?

### The Legal Foundation

**Regulation:** 8 CFR 214.2(o) explicitly permits agent-based petitions

**Definition:** An agent-petitioner is an organization (like IGTA) that files the O-1 visa petition on behalf of the beneficiary, rather than a single employer sponsoring the visa.

**How It's Different:**
- **Traditional O-1:** Employer sponsors you → You work only for that employer
- **Agent-Based O-1:** IGTA sponsors you → You work for ANY employer(s)

**Legal Basis Quote:**
> "A United States agent may file a petition in cases involving workers who are traditionally self-employed or who use agents to arrange short-term employment on their behalf with numerous employers." — 8 CFR 214.2(o)(2)(iv)(A)

### How It Works

1. **IGTA files as your "agent-petitioner"** with USCIS
2. **We create a "work itinerary"** describing your professional activities
3. **USCIS approves your O-1** with IGTA as petitioner
4. **You work for ANY employer(s)** that fit within your itinerary
5. **No refiling needed** when you change jobs or add employers
