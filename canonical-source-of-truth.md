## O-1 CRITERIA REFERENCE

**Requirement:** 3 of 8 criteria required
**Recommendation:** Build 5-6 for strong application

### The 8 Criteria

| # | Criterion | USCIS Description | Typical Timeline |
|---|-----------|-------------------|------------------|
| 1 | **Awards & Recognition** | Nationally or internationally recognized prizes or awards for excellence | 3-6 months to first award |
| 2 | **Membership in Elite Organizations** | Membership in associations requiring outstanding achievements for admission | 2-6 months depending on organization |
| 3 | **Published Material About You** | Published material in professional or major trade publications about you and your work | 2-4 months to first feature |
| 4 | **Judging the Work of Others** | Participation as a judge of the work of others in your field | 4-8 months (requires established reputation) |
| 5 | **Original Contributions** | Original contributions of major significance to your field | Ongoing throughout program |
| 6 | **Scholarly Articles** | Authorship of scholarly articles in professional journals or major media | Articles begin month 1 |
| 7 | **Critical or Essential Role** | Employment in critical or essential capacity for distinguished organizations | Ongoing throughout program |
| 8 | **High Salary or Remuneration** | Evidence of commanding high salary relative to others in field | Depends on career stage |
