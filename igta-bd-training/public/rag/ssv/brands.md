# BRAND GUIDE

## SSV (Sherrod Sports Visas) - Flagship

### Identity
- **Tagline**: "We solve problems for the best athletes, coaches, and organizations in the world"
- **Focus**: Sports visas - athletes, coaches, sports organizations
- **Primary Visas**: P-1, O-1A (athletics), EB-1A

### Domain & Links
- Main: www.sherrodsportsvisas.com
- Payment: www.sherrodsportsvisas.com/{amount}
- Engagement: www.sherrodsportsvisas.com/engagement{amount}
- Intake: https://sherrodsportsvisas.report

### Target Clients
- Professional athletes (all sports)
- Olympic/international competitors
- Coaches and trainers
- Sports organizations
- UFC/MMA fighters
- Tennis professionals

## IGTA (Innovative Global Talent Agency)

### Identity
- **Focus**: Entertainers, influencers, O-1B candidates, tech professionals
- **Primary Visas**: O-1A, O-1B, EB-1A

### Domain & Links
- Main: www.innovativeglobaltalent.com
- Petitioner: www.innovativeglobaltalent.com/2000
- Consultation: calendly.com/innovative_global_talent_agency/consultation

### Target Clients
- Entertainers and performers
- Social media influencers
- Tech professionals
- O-1B candidates

## AVA (Aventus Visa Agents)

### Identity
- **Focus**: Petitioner services, agent representation
- **Primary Service**: $2,000 petitioner service

### Domain
- Main: www.aventusvisaagents.com
- Payment: www.aventusvisaagents.com/2000

### Use For
- Standard sports petitioner cases
- Simple agent representation

## FRM (First Round Management)

### Special Relationship
- **P-1 Pricing**: $4,000 (not $6,000)
- They self-petition (no $2,000 petitioner service)
- Always honor this relationship

### Communication
"As a First Round Management client, you receive our special pricing: P-1 at $4,000 (regularly $6,000)."

## BRAND SELECTION GUIDE
- Athletes/coaches -> SSV
- Entertainers/influencers -> IGTA
- Simple petitioner -> AVA
- FRM client -> SSV at $4,000
