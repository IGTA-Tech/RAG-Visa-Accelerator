# VISA PROCESS GUIDE

## TIMELINE
- Standard processing: 3-6 months
- Premium processing: 15 business days ($2,805)

## CLIENT JOURNEY

### Stage 1: Initial Contact
- Lead contacts SSV
- BD sends intake form request
- Lead completes form at sherrodsportsvisas.report

### Stage 2: Evaluation
- Attorney reviews intake
- Assess visa eligibility
- Recommend visa type (P-1, O-1, EB-1A)

### Stage 3: Pricing & Engagement
- Send pricing based on visa type
- Client reviews terms
- Client signs engagement letter
- Client submits payment

### Stage 4: Document Collection
- Client receives checklist
- Client gathers evidence
- SSV reviews documents

### Stage 5: Filing
- SSV prepares petition
- File with USCIS
- Receive receipt notice

### Stage 6: Adjudication
- Wait for USCIS decision
- Respond to RFEs if needed
- Receive approval

### Stage 7: Visa Issuance
- Consular processing (if abroad)
- Interview
- Visa stamp issued

## PETITIONER SERVICE
- $2,000 additional
- Needed when client has no US employer
- Provided through AVA or IGTA
- FRM clients self-petition (don't need this)

## PREMIUM PROCESSING
- $2,805 (paid to USCIS)
- 15 business day guaranteed response
- Response can be: Approval, Denial, or RFE

## O-3 DEPENDENTS
- $1,000 per dependent
- Spouse and children of O-1 holder
- Applied after O-1 approval
- $205 consulate fee

## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: Standard 3-6 months. Premium 15 business days for $2,805.

**Q: Can my family come?**
A: Yes, O-1 holders bring spouse/children on O-3 ($1,000 each).

**Q: P-1 vs O-1?**
A: P-1 for specific events, tied to petitioner. O-1 more flexible, multiple employers, long-term.

**Q: What if denied?**
A: Review denial, consider appeal, motion to reopen, or new filing.

**Q: Do I need a petitioner?**
A: Yes, unless you have a US employer or are FRM client (self-petition).
