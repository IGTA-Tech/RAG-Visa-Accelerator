# VISA ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

## P-1A VISA (Internationally Recognized Athlete)

### Who Qualifies
Athletes internationally recognized in their sport coming for a specific competition/event.

### Requirements
1. International recognition in sport
2. Specific US event/competition lined up
3. Petitioner (employer/agent) required

### Good Evidence
- National team representation
- Professional league play
- International competition results
- Rankings in sport
- Media coverage
- Awards and honors

### When to Recommend P-1 Over O-1
- Client has specific competition dates
- Short-term engagement
- Team sport with organized schedule

## O-1A VISA (Extraordinary Ability - Athletics/Sciences/Business)

### Must Meet 3+ of 8 Criteria
1. Awards - nationally/internationally recognized
2. Memberships - associations requiring outstanding achievement
3. Published material - media coverage about them
4. Judging - judged others' work
5. Original contributions - major significance
6. Scholarly articles - professional publications
7. High salary - relative to others in field
8. Critical role - essential for distinguished organizations

### When to Recommend O-1 Over P-1
- No specific competition lined up
- Want flexibility to work for multiple organizations
- Coaching, training, or consulting roles
- Significant social media following
- Entertainment crossover
- Long-term US presence desired

## O-1B VISA (Arts/Entertainment)

### Better For
- Social media influencers
- Entertainers, musicians, actors
- Content creators
- Athletes with entertainment presence (Netflix, major endorsements)

## EB-1A VISA (Green Card)

### Requirements
- Higher standard than O-1
- Must meet 3+ of 10 criteria
- At very top of field

### Benefits
- Permanent residence (green card)
- Path to citizenship
- Spouse can work on derivative visa

## ELIGIBILITY ASSESSMENT QUESTIONS

**For Athletes:**
1. International competition experience?
2. Upcoming US competitions/events?
3. National team representation?
4. Professional contracts or rankings?
5. Media coverage?

**For Coaches:**
1. Coached international-level athletes?
2. Certifications/degrees in sport?
3. Athletes achieved notable success?
4. Featured in publications?
5. US job offer or agency representation?

**Red Flags:**
- No international recognition
- Amateur level only
- No documented achievements
- No US opportunity

**Green Flags:**
- International competition
- Professional contracts
- Awards and honors
- Media coverage
- High rankings
- Clear US opportunity
