## H-1B EMPLOYER DEPENDENCY PROBLEMS

### You're Tied to One Employer

**H-1B Reality:**
- You can only work for sponsoring employer
- Changing jobs = new H-1B petition required
- Job loss = 60-day grace period to find new sponsor or leave US
- Your visa status is controlled by employer
- Layoffs are devastating for H-1B holders

### Recent Layoff Crisis Impact

**Tech Industry Layoffs (2023-2024):**
- 400,000+ tech workers laid off
- H-1B holders scrambling to find sponsors in 60 days
- Many forced to leave US despite years of contribution
- Families uprooted, careers derailed
- Children removed from schools mid-year

### The "Golden Handcuffs" Problem

- Can't negotiate salary effectively (employer holds visa power)
- Can't leave toxic workplace easily
- Can't start your own company
- Can't take consulting/freelance work
- Stuck waiting years for green card while tied to employer

## H-1B DURATION LIMITS

### The 6-Year Maximum

**H-1B Facts:**
- Initial term: 3 years
- One extension: 3 additional years
- **Maximum total: 6 years**
- After 6 years: Must leave US or have green card pending

### Green Card Backlogs

For Indian nationals:
- EB-2/EB-3 wait time: **80+ years** (not a typo)
- EB-1 wait time: Several years
- Many will never get green card through employment

**Result:** Brilliant professionals stuck in "H-1B jail" for years, then forced to leave.
