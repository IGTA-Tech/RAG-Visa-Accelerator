## The Deal Memo Structure (What We Need)

Based on our multiple employer model (example: https://www.innovativeglobaltalent.com/multiple-employer-form), we need:

### **Simple Interest Letter Template:**
```
Nexrizen recognizes [Candidate Name] as a highly skilled professional in:
- AI/ML deployment and productionization
- Full-stack development with modern frameworks
- Cloud architecture and scaling

We confirm our interest in engaging [Candidate] for potential contract work at 
$[XXX]/hour, starting from their O-1 approval date, for projects including but 
not limited to:
- AI tool deployment and optimization
- Parallel development on client projects
- Technical architecture consulting

This letter expresses interest only and creates no obligation for either party.

Period: From visa approval through [3 years]
Estimated hours: As needed basis
```

### **Key Points:**
- No actual work commitment required
- Rate just needs to be above minimum wage (suggest $75-150/hour range)
- Starts from visa approval (avoids unauthorized work issues)
- Can be used for unlimited candidates
