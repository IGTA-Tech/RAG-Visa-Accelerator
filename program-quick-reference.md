## COMMON QUESTIONS (Voice-Optimized Answers)

**Q: Can I work for multiple employers on O-1?**
A: Yes! Our agent-based O-1 lets you work for unlimited employers, freelance, consult, and even start your own business.

**Q: Is there a lottery for O-1?**
A: No. O-1 is 100% merit-based. No lottery, no cap, no annual limits.

**Q: Can I start a business on O-1?**
A: Yes! With our agent-based structure, you can be CEO of your own company while on O-1.

**Q: What's the difference between H-1B and O-1?**
A: H-1B has a lottery (14% odds), ties you to one employer, and has a $100K minimum salary. O-1 is merit-based, lets you work for multiple employers, and has no salary minimum.

**Q: How many criteria do I need for O-1?**
A: You need 3 out of 8 criteria. We recommend building 5-6 for a strong application.

**Q: What's premium processing?**
A: USCIS guarantees a decision within 15 business days instead of 4-6 months. Costs an extra $2,805.

**Q: Can I upgrade from Profile Builder to Filing Accelerator?**
A: Yes! You get $2,000 off plus credit for payments already made.

**Q: What's the Deal Memo Portal?**
A: Our platform connecting O-1 candidates with employers. Elite and Filing Accelerator members get free access.

**Q: Do you guarantee approval?**
A: No one can legally guarantee USCIS approval. Our 94% approval rate reflects our systematic approach and only filing strong cases.
