## BRAND SELECTION DECISION TREE

```
Is the client a professional athlete or coach?
├── YES → Use SSV
│   └── Do they need petitioner services?
│       ├── YES → SSV + AVA (or IGTA for complex cases)
│       └── NO → SSV only
│
└── NO → What type of professional?
    ├── Entertainer/Influencer → IGTA
    ├── Tech/AI Professional → Innovative Automations or IGTA
    └── Other Professional → IGTA
```

## SPECIAL RELATIONSHIPS

### First Round Management (FRM)
- **Special Pricing**: P-1 at $4,000 (not $6,000)
- **Petitioner**: They self-petition (no $2,000 service needed)
- **Recognition**: Always honor the FRM relationship
- **Communication**: Reference the partnership

### How to Identify FRM Clients
- Management company is First Round Management
- Fighter/athlete managed by FRM
- Previous communication mentions FRM relationship
- UFC Contender Series fighters often FRM

### FRM Communication Template
"As a First Round Management client, you receive our special pricing: P-1 at $4,000 (regularly $6,000)."
