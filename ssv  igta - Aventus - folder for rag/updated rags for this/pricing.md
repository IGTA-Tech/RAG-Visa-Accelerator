# SSV PRICING REFERENCE

## STANDARD SERVICE FEES

### P-1 Visa (Athletes with Competitions)
- **Standard Rate**: $6,000
- **First Round Management (FRM) Rate**: $4,000 (special relationship pricing)
- **With Petitioner Service**: $8,000 ($6,000 + $2,000 petitioner)

### O-1 Visa (Extraordinary Ability)
- **Standard Rate**: $8,000
- **With Petitioner Service**: $10,000 ($8,000 + $2,000 petitioner)
- **O-1A**: Sciences, education, business, athletics
- **O-1B**: Arts, motion picture, television

### EB-1A Visa (Green Card - Extraordinary Ability)
- **Standard Rate**: $8,000
- **With Support Services**: $10,000
- **Premium Package**: $12,000

### Petitioner Services
- **Standard Rate**: $2,000
- Available through: Aventus Visa Agents OR Innovative Global Talent Agency

### Dependent Visas
- **O-3 (Spouse/Children)**: $1,000 additional per dependent
- Consular processing after primary O-1 approval
- $205 consulate fee (paid separately)

## GOVERNMENT FEES (Paid Separately by Client)

### USCIS Filing Fees
- I-129 Petition: $460
- Fraud Prevention Fee: $500
- AILA Fee: $150
- **Total Standard Filing**: ~$1,110

### Premium Processing (Optional)
- **Fee**: $2,805
- **Timeline**: 15 business days guaranteed response
- Recommended for urgent cases

### Consular Fees
- DS-160 Application: $205
- Reciprocity fees vary by country

## SPECIAL PRICING SCENARIOS

### First Round Management (FRM) Clients
- P-1 at $4,000 (not $6,000)
- They self-petition (no petitioner service needed)
- Reference the relationship when communicating

### Group/Team Applications
- Base visa fee + $1,000 per additional member

### Promoter Consultant Package
- **Rate**: $2,500
- Includes: Full PDF preparation
- Promoter files directly with USCIS

### AI/Tech Internship Program
- **Monthly Credit**: $2,500/month toward O-1
- **Duration**: 4 months
- **Total Credit**: Up to $10,000 toward $8,000 O-1 fee
