## TEMPLATE CUSTOMIZATION RULES

### Always Include:
- Lead's first name
- Specific visa type (never generic "visa")
- Their sport/achievement if known
- Correct pricing for their situation
- All relevant links (payment, engagement, description)

### Always Check:
- FRM status (use $4,000 not $6,000 for P-1)
- Petitioner needs (add $2,000 if required)
- Brand (SSV vs IGTA vs Aventus links)
- Conversation history for context

### Tone Guidelines:
- Professional but approachable
- Confident in assessments
- Clear about pricing
- Add urgency when timeline exists
- Personal touch when possible
