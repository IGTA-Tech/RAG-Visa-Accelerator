# VISA ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

## P-1A VISA (Internationally Recognized Athlete)

### Who Qualifies
Athletes who are internationally recognized as outstanding in their sport and are coming to the US for a specific athletic competition, event, or performance.

### Key Requirements
1. **International Recognition**: Must be recognized at an international level
2. **Specific Event**: Must have a specific competition, season, or event lined up
3. **Petitioner Required**: Need a US employer/agent to file on their behalf

### Evidence That Strengthens P-1A Cases
- International competition participation
- National team representation
- Professional league contracts
- Rankings in sport
- Media coverage
- Awards and honors
- Significant prize money earned

### When to Recommend P-1A Over O-1
- Client has specific competition dates
- Short-term engagement (season/event)
- Team sport with organized schedule
- Less documented "extraordinary" achievements but clear international play

### P-1A Limitations
- Tied to specific petitioner/event
- Cannot freelance or change employers easily
- Must maintain athletic activity

## O-1A VISA (Extraordinary Ability - Athletics/Sciences/Business/Education)

### Who Qualifies
Individuals with extraordinary ability demonstrated by sustained national or international acclaim.

### The "Sustained Acclaim" Standard
Must meet at least 3 of 8 criteria:

1. **Awards**: Nationally/internationally recognized prizes or awards
2. **Memberships**: Membership in associations requiring outstanding achievement
3. **Published Material**: Media coverage about the individual's work
4. **Judging**: Participation as a judge of others' work in the field
5. **Original Contributions**: Original contributions of major significance
6. **Scholarly Articles**: Authorship of scholarly articles in professional journals
7. **High Salary**: High salary or remuneration relative to others in field
8. **Critical Role**: Performed in critical or essential capacity for distinguished organizations

### When to Recommend O-1 Over P-1
- No specific competition lined up
- Want flexibility to work for multiple organizations
- Coaching, training, or consulting roles
- Significant social media following/influence
- Entertainment crossover (content creation, media appearances)
- Long-term US presence desired

### O-1 Advantages
- 3-year initial validity
- Can work for multiple petitioners
- More flexible scope of work
- Spouse can apply for O-3

### Comparable Evidence Strategy
For athletes in non-traditional sports or roles (motorsports fabrication, specialized coaching), can demonstrate achievements through:
- Trade publications
- Industry recognition
- Client testimonials
- Portfolio of work
- Social media influence metrics

## O-1B VISA (Extraordinary Ability - Arts/Entertainment)

### Who Qualifies
Artists, entertainers, and creative professionals with extraordinary ability or achievement.

### Key Criteria (Meet 3+)
1. Leading/starring role in productions with distinguished reputation
2. National/international recognition through critical reviews or media
3. Leading role for organizations with distinguished reputation
4. Record of commercial or critically acclaimed successes
5. Recognition from organizations, critics, government agencies, experts
6. High salary relative to others in the field

### When to Recommend O-1B
- Entertainers, musicians, actors
- Social media influencers with large followings
- Content creators
- Athletes with significant entertainment presence (Netflix, major endorsements)
- Crossover figures (athlete + influencer)

### O-1B vs O-1A Decision
- High follower count + entertainment content → O-1B
- Pure athletic achievement → O-1A
- Both strong → Choose based on easier evidence path

## EB-1A VISA (Green Card - Extraordinary Ability)

### Who Qualifies
Individuals at the very top of their field seeking permanent residence.

### Requirements (Meet 3+ of 10 Criteria)
1. Lesser nationally/internationally recognized prizes or awards
2. Membership in associations requiring outstanding achievement
3. Published material about the alien in major media
4. Participation as judge of others' work
5. Original contributions of major significance
6. Authorship of scholarly articles
7. Display of work at artistic exhibitions
8. Leading/critical role for distinguished organizations
9. High salary relative to others
10. Commercial success in performing arts

### When to Recommend EB-1A
- Client wants permanent residence (green card)
- Strong documented achievement history
- Plan to remain in US long-term
- Can demonstrate "extraordinary" level clearly
- Spouse needs work authorization (EB-1A derivative can work)

### EB-1A Timeline Expectations
- Standard processing: 6-12 months
- Premium processing available: 15 business days for initial response
- Total process to green card: 12-18 months typical

## ELIGIBILITY ASSESSMENT QUESTIONS

### Quick Qualification Check - Ask These:

**For Athletes:**
1. Have you competed at international level?
2. Do you have upcoming US competitions or events?
3. Have you represented your national team?
4. Do you have professional contracts or rankings?
5. Have you received media coverage for your achievements?

**For Coaches/Trainers:**
1. Have you coached international-level athletes?
2. Do you have certifications or degrees in your sport?
3. Have your athletes achieved notable success?
4. Have you been featured in publications?
5. Do you have a job offer or agency representation in US?

**For Entertainers/Influencers:**
1. What is your social media following across platforms?
2. Have you been featured in major media?
3. Do you have commercial success (earnings, endorsements)?
4. Have you won awards or recognition?
5. What is your engagement rate?

### Red Flags (May Not Qualify)
- No international recognition
- Amateur level only
- No documented achievements
- No US opportunity lined up
- Recent start to career (insufficient track record)

### Green Flags (Strong Candidate)
- International competition/representation
- Professional contracts
- Awards and honors
- Media coverage
- High rankings
- Significant social following (for O-1B)
- Clear US opportunity
